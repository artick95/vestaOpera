The Vesta Orchestra and Opera Foundation
aims to help performing arts graduates

and is anchored upon the volunteers, employees and board members,
who have the desire to create a cultural legacy for Nigeria and West Africa.

Our History and Impact

The Vesta Orchestra was Founded in 2017 by Rosalyn Aninyei and a group of colleague musicians who saw the need to establish a world class orchestra, that would showcase the great talent available in the region.  So far, we have mainly been funded through donations from Vesta Violins, a Violin workshop owned by Rosalyn Aninyei.
