Oana Craciunescu-Ionescu

Concert Mistress

A violinist and co-founder of The Vesta Orchestra, Oana is a Physicist with a career in Information Technology. She began her career at IBM and then joined Oracle UK as Territory Sales Leader for Eastern Europe, Middle East and Africa.The job allowed her to work with a multitude of cultures, but it did not keep her away from her first passion, the violin.

Eight years ago she moved with her family to Nigeria where she continues to play the violin and teach young children violin.

We can do more for even more musicians with your support.
